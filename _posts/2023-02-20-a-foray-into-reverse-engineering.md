Today I got two invoices from my hosting provider all-inkl. Apparently they are sending out their invoices as HTML now and I should save the file to my computer and open it. And I did. Like an idiot. The browser window opened and showed a login form and it hit me: it's a phishing mail.

![all-inkl phishing mail attachment](/media/images/reverse_engineering/all-inkl.png)

Adrenaline kicked my tired brain into gear and by reflex I hit `cmd-w`. But how bad was it? Did I get pawned? How sophisticated was the malware? Did it exploit a Safari bug and gain persistence on my system? I rolled up my sleeves and dug in.
